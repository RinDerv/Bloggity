The Best Movies According to Me

My favorite movies of all-time

Neon Genesis Evangelion | Trailer | Favorite OST

"Neon Genesis Evangelion: Thrice Upon a Time" stands out as one of the greatest animated movies of all time due to its compelling narrative, deep psychological exploration, and stunning animation. The film masterfully concludes the iconic Evangelion series, addressing complex themes of identity, human connection, and existentialism. Its intricate character development and thought-provoking symbolism contribute to its status as a landmark in animated storytelling. The movie's emotional resonance, combined with its visually arresting sequences, cements its place among the best animated films, offering a profound and unforgettable cinematic experience.

La La Land | Trailer | Favorite OST

a cinematic masterpiece that earns its status as one of the greatest movies ever made. With its enchanting musical numbers, captivating performances by Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone, and visually stunning direction by Damien Chazelle, the film seamlessly blends nostalgia with modern storytelling. The movie's homage to classic Hollywood musicals, coupled with its contemporary narrative about love and artistic aspirations, creates a timeless and resonant experience. "La La Land" is a celebration of the magic of cinema, combining exceptional craftsmanship, a compelling story, and a soundtrack that lingers long after the credits roll.

Inception | Trailer | Favorite OST

Inception stands as one of the greatest movies ever made, thanks to Christopher Nolan's visionary direction, a mind-bending and original concept, and stellar performances. The film's intricate narrative, exploring dreams within dreams, challenges and captivates the audience in equal measure. With groundbreaking visual effects, a gripping score, and a thought-provoking exploration of reality and perception, "Inception" elevates the science fiction genre. Nolan's meticulous storytelling and the film's ability to engage both intellectually and emotionally contribute to its status as a cinematic masterpiece, leaving a lasting impact on audiences worldwide.

Spider-Man | Trailer | Favorite OST

"Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" is a groundbreaking animated film that earns its place among the greatest. With its innovative animation style, vibrant visuals, and a fresh take on the superhero genre, it offers a unique and visually stunning experience. The film's engaging storyline, diverse characters from various Spider-Universes, and a perfect blend of humor and heart make it a standout in animated storytelling. "Spider-Verse" not only pays homage to the comic book roots of Spider-Man but also pushes the boundaries of animation, delivering a dynamic and memorable cinematic achievement.
